# Product Overview

Jobfinder is a full-stack AI-powered job search assistant. It helps users:

- Upload and parse resumes using a local Ollama LLM to extract structured profile data
- Scrape job listings from multiple sources (Indeed, Naukri, Internshala, Google Jobs, JSearch API)
- Match jobs against the user's skills and preferences
- Send bulk outreach emails to recruiters with contact emails extracted from job descriptions
- Manage user profiles with skills, experience, education, and projects

The system is designed for individual job seekers who want to automate their job hunt pipeline — from resume parsing through job discovery to recruiter outreach.
